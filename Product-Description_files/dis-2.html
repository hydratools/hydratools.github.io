DOUBLE PUMP 100 TON HYDRAULIC COMPRESSOR 'HMT' MAKE:-


(More economical, faster than single pump & long life of high pressure pump) Monoblock Compressor is designed to apply compression fittings to ACSR having diameters from 14.15mm to 39.24mm and Ground Wire of all size. It is compact in design and sturdy make cylinder body is made out of high tensile solid steel.Its ram is made ut of alloy steel, duly hardened and ground finished to wear resistance and is rendered oil tight with canvas impregnated rubber oil seal. thee am is lowered automatically by strong tension springs when pressure release valve opened.

Oil Reservoir in the lowest part of the compressor body is made out of alloyed aluminium with oil lever screw and filters fitted on a steel platform. Four folding carrying handles are hinged to the frame of compressor.

Valve Assembly is at the front of the compressor. Low pressure high speed pup is at left and high pressure pump at righ side, both are single acting plunger pumps. Pressure release hand wheel fitted with conical point alve of special steel hardened and ground is at right side and it touches gently to its seat without rotation by closing hand wheel. As such, the release valve never damages ti its seat. Pressure gauge is at left backside to the release valve.

There is also spring loaded safety valve provided in the centre of the two pumps which acts to prevent over loading. The safety valve can be set at any desired pressure with the help of pressure gauge by screwing of its threaded bolt wit lock nut fitted on the top of safety valve body. Safety valve is pre-set in the factory, generally at 115-120 ton during testing of the compressure.

Important components of the machine such as main ram and plunger pumps are manufactured from alloy steel duly hardened and surface ground finished with close controlled tolerance for wear-resistane and long life. Other parts like hinge pins, links are also heat treated for long life.
